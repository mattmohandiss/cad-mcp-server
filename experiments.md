   least 80% should describe a painful, manual
   workflow built around screenshots, PDFs, or
   repeated clarification.

2. In 4 weeks, show pricing to 10-15 qualified
   prospects; at least 30% should choose a tier
   or agree to a follow-up buying conversation.

3. In 5 weeks, interview at least 5 prospects per
   segment and score pain, urgency, and
   workflow frequency; one segment should
   clearly outscore the others.

4. In 4 weeks, show competitor framing to 10-15
   target prospects; at least 50% should prefer
   a packet-first workflow for async review.

5. In 4 weeks, survey 15-20 target teams; at
   least 70% should report communicating
   revisions weekly to monthly.
